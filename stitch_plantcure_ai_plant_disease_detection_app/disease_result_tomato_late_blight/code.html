info

Late blight is caused by the fungus-like organism Phytophthora infestans. It spreads rapidly in cool, wet weather. Immediate removal of infected plants is recommended.

Affected Areas

Primarily leaves and fruit. Stems may show secondary infection leading to plant collapse.

Spread Speed

Very High. Can destroy a whole field within 7-10 days if left untreated.
